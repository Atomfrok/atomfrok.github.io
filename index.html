Flipe - In this digital era, gadgets have become one of the items whose value needs are fairly important, especially smartphones and laptops. Ideally, smartphones can become more flexible because they are used for everyday needs. But laptops are no less important and become the main object to support our work. Especially now that almost everyone is using a laptop for work.


Nowadays, laptops are a necessity for every worker. So it is very important to pay attention to the quality of existing laptops. So, here's a selection of good laptops for work that you can buy.


1.Lenovo Ideapad 320-14IKB


The first laptop series that has good quality is the Lenovo Ideapad 320-14IKB. With a screen size of 14 inches, this laptop has good graphics, namely the nVidia GT920 MX. This laptop is also suitable for use for various work matters because it is equipped with an Intel Core i5 7200U- 2.5 GHz processor.


The product from the Lenovo Ideapad 320-14IKB is equipped with 4GB of RAM so that it allows it to work properly. Compared to the previous Lenovo laptop series, the Lenovo Ideapad 320-14IK has a relatively long lifespan due to good electricity usage efficiency so that the laptop battery lasts longer. Having an elegant design, clear audio, and a keyboard that is comfortable to use makes this laptop the perfect choice for doing your daily work.


2. ASUS ZenBook 14


In Indonesia, laptops that are often found and used by workers and students are laptops with the Asus brand. Asus has issued various series of products for every consumer need with different types. One of Asus' newest flagship releases is the Asus ZenBook Flip 14.


The Asus ZenBook Flip 14 claims to be an elegant and lightweight laptop that combines outstanding performance and easy portability. This laptop also has a charming appearance to meet all your gadget needs. Powered by the revolutionary ASUS ScreenPad2 feature to increase productivity and also equipped with an 11th Gen Intel Core processor. This laptop will certainly meet all your needs and is very flexible for all uses.


3. Dell Vostro


Good laptop choices also come from the Dell brand. The various laptop series produced by Dell are in the premium class but are offered at affordable prices. Generally, workers or business people use a lot of Dell laptops with the Vostro type because they have the ideal size but are still light. This portable laptop is designed to boost Dell consumer productivity.


Key features designed in this Dell Vostro laptop include an advanced processor to ensure faster performance, an anti-glare display, multiple features to connect easily to other devices, a built-in HD webcam and noise-reducing Waves MaxxAudio Pro. background and enhance audio clarity. Not to forget that this laptop is equipped with high-speed Gigabit Ethernet all packed into a professional, thin and light design for easy portability.

4.Lenovo Yoga 7i


Furthermore, if you are looking for a 2-in-1 laptop with an 11th Generation Intel Core i7 processor then this Lenovo Yoga 7i can be a consideration. This Lenovo laptop series delivers the perfect combination of responsiveness, long-lasting battery life, and stunning visuals. You can enjoy a superior audio-visual experience and intelligent AI convenience.


The Lenovo Yoga 7i provides the best experience for living and working anywhere as it combines versatile portability and enhanced productivity. You can also enjoy slick quality visuals on a 14-inch FHD screen with a thin design optimized with Intel Iris Xegraphics. Combined with front-facing Dolby Atmos Speaker System, the Lenovo Yoga 7i offers entertainment and is a great laptop choice while supporting better work anywhere and anytime.


5. HP Pavilion


The HP Pavilion is powered by the 11th Generation Intel i7 which can be considered to be a work laptop. The HP Pavilion 14 laptop packs more performance so it allows you to work more flexibly anywhere and anytime.


This laptop has a 15.6-inch Full HD screen and comes with 16GB of RAM and 1TB of SSD storage which is integrated with Windows 10. The design of this laptop is packaged very attractively and elegantly, with a thin, light appearance and equipped with a metal body. You will be more confident to carry this laptop anywhere. The laptop also features a fingerprint scanner and audio by Bang & Olufsen.
